General variables:
var 0x8000: Max berry yield for tree
var 0x8001: Miniumum berry yield for tree
var 0x8005: Current stage
var 0x8006: Time planted
var 0x8007: Was watered
var 0x8008: Times watered
var 0x8009: ID of berry


RAM Data storage:
[Current Stage: 1 byte][Time planted: 2 bytes][Watered: 1 byte][times watered: 1 byte][ID of plant: 1 bytes]
